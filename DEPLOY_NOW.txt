DEPLOYMENT COMMAND:
-------------------
python deploy.py


WHAT YOU NEED:
--------------
1. Your Lute Wallet mnemonic (25 words)
2. At least 0.5 ALGO in your TestNet wallet
3. Get TestNet ALGO from: https://bank.testnet.algorand.network/


WHAT WILL HAPPEN:
-----------------
1. Script will prompt for your mnemonic
2. Contract will be compiled
3. Contract will be deployed to TestNet
4. Contract will be funded with 0.5 ALGO
5. You'll get contract.json with App ID and Address
6. Ready for frontend integration!


AFTER DEPLOYMENT:
-----------------
You will receive:
  ✓ App ID (unique contract identifier)
  ✓ App Address (contract's Algorand address)
  ✓ contract.json (for frontend integration)
  ✓ contract-abi.json (ABI only)
  ✓ Explorer link to view your contract


FRONTEND INTEGRATION:
---------------------
1. Copy contract.json to your React project
2. Copy frontend-integration.ts to your React project
3. Use the BountyBoard class in your components
4. Connect with @txnlab/use-wallet
